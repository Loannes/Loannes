I Like Box - Support

About the App

"I Like Box" is a mobile game where a tuxedo cat embarks on an adventure to find boxes. Help the cat overcome various obstacles, defeat enemies, and find the beloved box!

Support Information

If you encounter any issues while using the app or have questions, please contact us through the methods below.

Contact Methods

Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your account, you can do so from the app by going to "Settings > Account Management > Delete Account." If you need further assistance, please contact us via email.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. I can't delete my account.

Please check the "Delete Account" button in the app settings. If the issue persists, contact our support team via email.

2. The game won't start.

Try updating the app to the latest version and restart it. If the problem continues, reach out to us via email.

3. My game progress is not saved.

Please make sure you are logged in and playing in a stable network environment.
