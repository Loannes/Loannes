Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the developer of the app "I Like Box" (hereinafter referred to as “we”, “our”, or “the app”) collects, uses, and protects your personal information. We are committed to protecting your data and complying with applicable privacy laws.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address (collected during social login)
  • Game scores and ranking data

2. How We Collect Information

  • When you log in using social accounts (Apple, Google, etc.)
  • Automatically during gameplay (e.g., score and ranking data)

3. Purpose of Use

  • User identification and login functionality
  • Saving and displaying game scores and rankings
  • Improving game features and user experience
  • Providing and analyzing personalized advertisements

4. Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described above. Upon user request or account deletion, your information will be deleted without delay unless retention is required by law.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to process data on our behalf:

  • Google AdMob
    Purpose: Advertising and ad performance analytics
    Data Provided: Ad ID and other non-personal data
  • Supabase
    Purpose: User data and game score storage
    Data Provided: Email address, game records

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. Please contact us via email or use in-app functions if available.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 14. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Data Protection Measures

  • Secure data storage using Supabase
  • Access control and limited data permissions
  • Protection against unauthorized access or data leaks
